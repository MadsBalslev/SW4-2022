    # Pasta
### Ingredients
- Pasta (100g)
- Water (1l)
- Salt (10g)
- Pot
- Stove
- Sifter
### Steps
1. Put Water in Pot
2. With Stove Heat the content in Pot bring to boil
3. Put Pasta in Pot
4. Put Salt in Pot
5. Stop when boil pasta for 6-10 minutes
6. Put content in Pot in Sifter
7. Sift content in Sifter with Sifter
8. serve the content in Sifter
