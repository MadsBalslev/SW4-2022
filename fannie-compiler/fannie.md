# Pasta
### Ingredients
- Pasta (100g,25whole)
- Water (1l)
- Salt (10g)
### Tools
- Pot
- Stove
- Sifter
### Steps
1. Put Water in Pot
2. With Stove Heat the content in Pot Bring water to boil.
3. Put Pasta in Pot
4. Put Salt in Pot
5. Stop when The pasta has boiled for 6-10 minutes or is al dente.
6. Sift content in Pot with Sifter
7. serve the content in Sifter
